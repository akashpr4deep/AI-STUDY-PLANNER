# Problem Statement
Many students face difficulty in managing their study time properly, especially when they have multiple subjects with different difficulty levels. In most cases, students are not able to decide which subject needs more focus, and they may spend equal time on all subjects or ignore the ones they are weak in. This leads to inefficient study planning and can affect their overall performance. There is a need for a system that can help students plan their study time in a smarter way by considering both the difficulty of subjects and their performance in each subject. The problem is to design a simple and effective solution that can take these factors into account and generate a proper study timetable which helps students focus more on weaker areas and manage their time better.


# Scope of the Project
The scope of this project is to help students plan their daily study schedule in a simple and effective way. It takes basic inputs like subjects, difficulty levels, marks, and study hours to generate a suitable timetable. The project is limited to a command-line interface and focuses only on daily planning. However, it can be improved in future by adding more features like weekly schedules, better user interface, and advanced AI methods.

# Target Users
The main target users of this project are students who want to manage their study time more effectively. It is especially useful for school and college students who have multiple subjects and find it difficult to decide how much time to give to each subject. It can also be helpful for beginners who want a simple and easy way to create a study timetable based on their performance and difficulty level.


# High-Level Features
The main high level features of this project include taking user input such as subjects, difficulty levels, marks, and total study hours, and then processing this data to create a personalized study plan. The system identifies weak subjects and gives them more priority while allocating study time. It generates a clear and structured timetable with proper time slots and short breaks. The program also displays the timetable on the screen and saves it in a file for later use. Overall, it provides a simple and effective way to manage study time.
